Training Splits

Discover different training splits and find the one that fits your goals. It is recommended that you train each muscle twice a week for optimal muscle growth, however, ensure you are planning your training days so you get the adequate rest

Full-Body Split

Example:
Day 1:Chest, Back, Shoulders, Arms, Legs
Day 2: Rest
Day 3: Rest
Day 4: Chest, Back, Shoulders, Arms, Legs
REPEAT

Upper/Lower Split

Example:
Day 1: Upper Body (Chest, Back, Shoulders, Arms)
Day 2: Rest
Day 3: Lower Body (Legs)
Day 4: Rest

Push/Pull/Legs Split

Example:
Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps)
Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)
Day 4: Rest

Arnold Split
