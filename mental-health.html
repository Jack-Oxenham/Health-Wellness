Mental Health & Wellness

Mental health is a vital component of overall well-being. This section highlights the importance of mental health, how wellness practices can help, and provides tools to track your mood and find resources.

Benefits of Mental Health Practices

  • Exercise: Reduces stress and improves mood.
  • Nutrition: Supports brain health and emotional balance.
  • Mindfulness: Enhances focus and emotional resilience.
  • Sleep: Boosts cognitive function and reduces anxiety.
